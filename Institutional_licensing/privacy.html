Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Overview

FORTA Technologies ("FORTA", "we", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of information submitted through this institutional licensing framework. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect data submitted via the Expression of Interest form and general site interaction.

By submitting information through this site, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to assess and process institutional licensing inquiries. This includes:

  • Organization name — full legal entity name
  • Organization type — classification of the submitting entity
  • Jurisdiction — geographic region of operation
  • Infrastructure category — relevant sector classification
  • Deployment scope — intended scale of technology deployment
  • Capital allocation range — indicative budget band
  • Strategic objective — brief description of intended use
  • Institutional contact email — for correspondence only

We do not collect personal identification documents, financial account details, or any data beyond what is listed above through this form.

3. How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through the EOI form is used exclusively for:

  • Assessing eligibility for FORTA institutional licensing
  • Communicating qualification outcomes and term sheet issuance
  • Internal records related to the licensing engagement

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your submitted information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage & Security

Form submissions are processed through Formspree, a third-party form handling service, and are transmitted via HTTPS. FORTA Technologies stores qualifying submissions in secured internal systems with access limited to the institutional licensing team.

Submissions that do not progress to a licensing engagement are deleted within 90 days of submission.

5. Spam Protection

This site uses Cloudflare Turnstile for automated spam prevention. Turnstile operates without presenting CAPTCHA challenges and processes minimal browser signals to distinguish human from automated submissions. Cloudflare's privacy policy governs data processed by the Turnstile widget.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of information you have submitted. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days.

For submissions governed by GDPR (European Union) or India's DPDP Act, we honor all applicable statutory rights.

7. Cookies & Tracking

This site does not use analytics tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or any third-party tracking beyond what is necessary for form spam protection (Cloudflare Turnstile). No persistent identification cookies are set by FORTA Technologies.

8. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact the FORTA Technologies data team at:
privacy@fortatechnologies.com
